About BTS

BTS is a South Korean boy group formed in 2013 and consists of seven members: RM, Jin, SUGA, j-hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Known for their powerful messages and genre-defying music, BTS has inspired millions around the world. Despite their global fame, the members consistently show appreciation and love for their fans, ARMY.








From the beginning, BTS has created a wide range of merchandise—photo cards, plushies, and BT21 characters. A little too much, some might say. ARMYs love collecting these items, but some fans have joked that their BTS merch gets worn out fast from all the love and handling. Now, we see more durable, collectible-grade items being released to keep up with demand.

Although BTS is known for their intense performances, the members cherish peaceful moments together. They often rest on the studio floor, pile into one room at night, or lean on one another during long rehearsals. These small moments show their close bond and how they find comfort through each other.





The first time BTS appeared on stage, they were nervous and uncertain. But they gave it their all, even if it meant sweating through hours of dance practice and multiple outfit changes.
Now, even with years of experience, they still go through intense preparation before every performance. From styling and choreography to vocal warmups, every comeback includes days of prep that fans never see.






How BTS Prepares for a Comeback

...and how they power through the pressure



  1. Concept Development
    • Each BTS era starts with a clear message or theme. The company and members brainstorm visuals, moods, and lyrical content.
    • Once the theme is confirmed, the album cover, styling, and even music video scenes are crafted around that concept.
  2. Rehearsals
    • The group rehearses choreography for hours daily. Even with years of experience, they treat each performance like it's their first.
    • They often review dance clips and give each other feedback to improve timing and expressions.
  3. Recording Sessions
    • Vocal lines are recorded separately, with members layering harmonies and adjusting melodies to match emotion and energy.
    • Producers like SUGA or RM may tweak production details until the mix feels just right.
  4. Promotion Planning
    • From teaser trailers to fan meetings and V Lives, every interaction is planned to connect with ARMY.
    • Fans eagerly anticipate TikToks, photo drops, and behind-the-scenes content.
  5. Live Performances
    • The final product is the comeback stage—where visuals, vocals, and emotions explode in sync.
    • It’s a reward for all the hard work and a love letter to ARMY.

In just three weeks they build entire eras that live forever in our hearts!
